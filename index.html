Prague Shooting Club

Prague shooting club provides shooting and firearms-related events in Prague since 1995. We have almost 30 years of experience in organizing fun and safe events for everyone who is willing to support functioning of this club with a few options of donations.

For a small donation we can offer you a one-time participation in our basic level events
Medium donation grants you 3 permission to participate in 3 basic level events and one VIP
Big donation allows to participate in any of our events of any level for a year
